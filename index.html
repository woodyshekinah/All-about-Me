All About Me

My Background

Shekinah Woody is a 17 year old girl who is positive-oriented and enthusiastic.She was born and raised in Kenya,Kakamega County.She started her primary school education at the age of two at Kitale School Primary where she completed her KPCE in 2019 and excelled with 421 marks.Thereafter she got the chance to join The Alliance Girls High School where she sat her KCSE in 2023 and got an A-.

Why did I join the DirectED program?

I am a philomath and therefore enjoy learning new skills.When the opportunity was presented to me I was so excited and quickly signed up to enrol.I am aware that coding is a skill that not many people in Kenya have and there is therefore a high demand for people with coding skills.I had already asked my parents to facilitate me to take coding classes immediately after high school but it was not possible because it was too expensive and out of their budget.The fact that DirectED is an organization that equips us with these skills for free made my dream possible and I therefore decided to join this program instead of idling around at home and I have absolutely no regrets!.I am more than grateful to the DirectED founders for this opportunity because without them I would have been at home wishing that my parents had more money to facilitate my coding classes.
