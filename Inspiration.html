Who they are

Mystical, transcendent, intuitive. These are all words that describe INIKO, the multitalented singer, songwriter, and instrumentalist from Brooklyn, New York. INIKO, whose pronouns are they/them.They first captured the public's attention with their 2022 song "The King's Affirmation." Inspired by Nicki Minaj's "Do We Have a Problem?" the track immediately went viral, receiving over 9 million YouTube views and earning INIKO a deal with Columbia Records. Their success continued last year with the release of "Jericho," a song that was widely remixed and covered on TikTok with over 27 million views on YouTube. Their emotional and emotive songs, powerful vocals, and eclectic style have made them a rising favorite among listeners and a standout in the alternative soul space.
